We’re nerds who think conversations are important. And we’re on a mission to prove that mass communication can be meaningful. It all started with one question: How can we create technology that brings us together instead of isolating us? That question led to one more: How has digital communication failed us – as individuals, organizations and small businesses? Which brings us some soon-to-be relics of the past: spam mail and email marketing campaigns. When we get spam, there’s a reason we don’t write back. It’s a one-way conversation that keeps the consumer on mute. We craved a solution that does what small businesses and leaders already do better than the big guys: Listen to your community and connect with like-minded people who help you grow. And we wanted that technology to be accessible to people without millions in their marketing budget. That’s why Irrigate Messaging lets you connect with your community on apps they’re already using more than email. And if connecting with them means making it easier to make appointments, buy products, talk to you or do pretty much anything else in 3 minutes on Messenger, we’ll build that for you.
